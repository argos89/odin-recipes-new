Avocado Toast

Description

Avocado toast makes for a filling, nutritious breakfast loaded with healthy fats. This recipe is simple, leaving room for additions and modifications.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Two slices of your bread of choice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • A squeeze from half a lemon
  • Optional toppings: parsley, cilantro, parmesan cheese, red pepper flakes, or arugula

Steps

  1. Toast your bread to desired toastiness
  2. Cut avocado in half and smear across the toast, one half of the avocado per slice of bread
  3. Top with a squeeze of lemon juice and salt and pepper to taste
  4. Add additional toppings as desired. I love arugula and parmesan cheese on avocado toast.
