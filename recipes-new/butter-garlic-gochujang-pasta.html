Butter Garlic Gochujang Pasta

Description

A delightfully buttery, garlicky, spicy pasta that only takes 15 minutes to make. The perfect meal for a lazy weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp Gochujang Chile Paste
  • 1 pound of pasta of your choice. I use Barilla Chickpea Rotini for added protein.
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 tbsp butter

Steps

    Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Be sure to salt the pasta water.
    In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium-high heat. Once the butter is fully melted, add the garlic.
    Once the garlic is fragrant (in about 10 seconds), add the gochujang paste and stir until the butter, garlic, and gochujang are fully combined. Remove from heat.
    Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss in the gochujang sauce.
    Enjoy!
